MARY POPPINS

AS THE PIONEER TO THE ENGINE-LESS FLYING TECHNIQUES, MARY POPPINS WAS A TRULY REMARKABLE ARTIST. WHEN UNSCHEDULED TO BABYSIT AS A NANNY, HER HOURS WERE DEVOTED TO THE ART OF UMBRELLA FLYING. THE CAUSE TO HER UNFORTUNATE DEATH WAS UNKNOWN, BUT SOME BLAMED THE POWERLINES.
